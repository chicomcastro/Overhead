Tower defense game — you are the Wizard. Defend your tower from waves of knights trying to rescue the princess. Build magical spheres along the path to stop them.

## Play

- **Web demo:** play directly at [chicomcastro.github.io/Overhead](https://chicomcastro.github.io/Overhead/) — works on desktop and mobile, installable as PWA, playable offline.
- **Download:** available on [itch.io](https://chicomcastro.itch.io/overhead).

## About

Overhead was a National finalist at the Brazilian Games Symposium 2017 (SBGames) in Best Game, Best Student Game and Best Technology.

Originally developed for CIG 2017, the [ITA](http://www.ita.br/) internal game development competition, where it won first place. It was also the only student game selected in the Best Game category at SBGames 2017.
